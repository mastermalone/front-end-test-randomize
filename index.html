Favorite Animal

My favorite animal is none other than the lion. These majestic creatures live in large social groups called prides. This social behavior is unlkike any of other the big cats that live in the wild. When it comes time for the hunt, the players in charge are usually the female members of the pride. What's interesting about these cats is the teamwork involved when hunting their prey.

They will often corral thier prey into an abush while other members of the pride lie in wait for the kill

What makes this creature special to me is that it is also the animal associated with the Zodiac sign, Leo. I happen to be a leo so it's a no-brainer.
